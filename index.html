Immigrant & Refugee Resources

A curated list for working with immigrant and refugee populations.

National Organizations

Migration Health Initiative (MHI)

MHI provides practical resources, trainings, and workforce development to support the health of people experiencing migration.

Learn more →

U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants (USCRI)

This organization provides direct services and support for refugees and immigrants, including a focus on mental health.

Learn more →

Local Nashville Organizations

Nashville International Center for Empowerment (NICE)

NICE assists refugees and immigrants with case management, health education, and culturally competent mental health services.

Learn more →

Siloam Health

This clinic provides holistic health services for immigrants and refugees in Nashville and utilizes community health workers.

Learn more →

Tennessee Justice for Our Neighbors (TNJFON)

TNJFON provides free or low-cost, high-quality immigration legal services to low-income immigrants, refugees, and asylum seekers.

Learn more →

Catholic Charities, Diocese of Nashville

Their Refugee Resettlement Program offers a wide range of services for newly arrived refugees, including case management and employment support.

Learn more →

Nations Ministry Center

A community-based organization that provides low-cost immigration services, educational programs, and youth programming.

Learn more →

Conexión Américas

Assists Latino families with social, civic, and economic integration, offering services that build community and empower individuals.
